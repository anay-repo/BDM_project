About New Golden Watch Shop

Store Overview:

  • Type: Family-run B2C watch & clock retail shop
  • Location: Railway Road, Rewari, Haryana
  • Established: 1957
  • Products: Wrist watches & wall clocks (Titan, Ajanta, Sonata, etc.)
  • Operations: 9 AM - 9 PM (Mon–Sat)

Problem Statement & Objectives

Key Challenges Identified:

  • Dead Stock Accumulation: Premium brands are accumulating unsold inventory, tying up capital and space.
  • Profit Stagnation: Average weekly profit is stagnant around ₹20,777, limiting growth.
  • Manual Record-Keeping: Data is recorded in a Khata Book, preventing systematic analysis.

Project Goal:

Analyze sales data from Feb–Mar 2025 to provide actionable recommendations to reduce dead stock by 40-50% and improve weekly profits by 20-25%.

  • Format: Cleaned, aggregated (Excel/Sheets)

Analytical Approach

Descriptive Stats

Calculated totals and averages to identify top sellers like Titan & Ajanta.

Trend Analysis

Revealed that Mondays & Fridays are peak sales days.

ABC Segmentation

Classified inventory into A (High-Value), B, and C (Low-Value) categories.

Dead Stock Analysis

Tracked unsold items to flag slow movers like Tommy Hilfiger.

Results & Key Findings

Total Units Sold: Wrist Watches

(Feb–Mar 2025)

Key Insight: Titan (135 units), Sonata (89), and Zoop (86) are the clear market leaders. In contrast, premium brands like Tommy Hilfiger (10) sell in very low volumes.

Total Units Sold: Wall Clocks

(Feb–Mar 2025)

Key Insight: Ajanta (116 units) is the dominant brand, nearly doubling the sales of the next competitor. Citizen (29) is a significant underperformer.

Daily Sales Performance

(Aggregated Units Sold by Day)

Key Insight: Sales consistently peak at the start and end of the week, with Monday and Friday being the most productive days, presenting an opportunity for targeted promotions.

Dead Stock Inventory Trend

(Unsold Units Over Time)

Key Insight: The inventory for Tommy Hilfiger and Citizen steadily increases, confirming they are becoming dead stock. In contrast, the inventory of a fast-mover like Titan is effectively managed.

Weekly Profit Trend

(Feb–Mar 2025)

Key Insight: The weekly profit is largely stable but stagnant, fluctuating around a mean of ₹20,777. This indicates a lack of growth, which targeted strategies can address.

ABC Analysis (Inventory Impact)

(Classification by Revenue Contribution)

Key Insight: Category A items generate most revenue and require priority restocking. Category C items contribute little but hold significant stock value, making them prime candidates for clearance.

Actionable Recommendations

To Reduce Dead Stock and Optimize Capital:

RecommendationRationale & ActionPriority
Adopt Data-Driven RestockingReduce orders for Category C items (Tommy Hilfiger, Citizen). Re-allocate budget to Category A (Titan, Ajanta).High
Run Clearance SalesOffer a 20-30% discount on aged C-Category stock to liquidate inventory and improve cash flow immediately.High
Create Bundle OffersPair slow-movers with best-sellers (e.g., "Buy a Titan watch, get ₹500 off a Tommy Hilfiger"). This clears stock while protecting margins.Medium
Enhance Store LayoutMove top-sellers to the front. Create a dedicated clearance section.Medium

To Break Profit Stagnation:

RecommendationRationale & ActionPriority
Target Peak Sales DaysIntroduce special promotions or "Deal of the Day" offers on Mondays and Fridays to capitalize on high footfall.High
Digitize Sales RecordsTransition from the Khata Book to Google Sheets for easy, ongoing analysis and forecasting.High
Expand OnlinePartner with platforms like Amazon or Flipkart to reach a wider audience for premium, slow-moving brands.Medium
